The Process

How Aerial Monitoring Works

A structured, repeatable process designed to keep you informed about the condition of your property — without disruption to your daily life.

01

Enrollment & Property Review

We begin with a consultation to understand your property, priorities, and monitoring objectives. Your home is added to our scheduled coverage route with a tailored inspection protocol.

02

Scheduled Aerial Visits

On your selected schedule, our operators conduct a thorough drone flight over your property. Roofline, perimeter, drainage areas, and structures are systematically documented using high-resolution imagery.

03

Documented Report Delivery

Following each visit, you receive an organized visual report with annotated observations, comparison imagery from prior visits, and any items flagged for your attention — all delivered discreetly to your inbox.
