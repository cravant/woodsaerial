What We Offer

Aerial Monitoring Services

Each program is designed to provide a specific level of oversight — from ongoing monthly monitoring to focused event-based inspections.

Ongoing Program

Monthly Property Monitoring

Consistent, scheduled aerial oversight of your property throughout the year. Monthly monitoring is our most comprehensive program and is particularly valuable for second homes, vacant estates, and properties in areas prone to seasonal weather events.

Each monthly visit follows a structured flight path covering all primary structures, rooflines, drainage areas, driveways, and exterior features. A documented report with annotated imagery is delivered to you within 48 hours of each visit.

  • One scheduled aerial visit per month
  • High-resolution imagery of all primary structures
  • Roofline and perimeter documentation
  • Drainage and grading observation notes
  • Comparison view from prior visits when available
  • Annotated PDF report delivered by email
  • Secure image archive with 12-month retention
  • Priority scheduling following severe weather events

Event-Based

Post-Storm Inspection Reports

Following severe weather events — hail, high winds, heavy rain, or ice storms — a prompt aerial inspection provides the documentation you need to assess property damage, initiate insurance claims, or make informed decisions about repairs.

Post-storm inspections are conducted within 48 to 72 hours of a weather event and focus specifically on areas most vulnerable to storm-related damage. The resulting report is structured to support insurance documentation requirements.

  • Inspection conducted within 48–72 hours of storm event
  • Focused coverage of roofline and all roof surfaces
  • Documentation of gutters, fascia, and exterior structures
  • Damage identification and annotation on all imagery
  • Comparison against prior visit imagery (if enrolled)
  • Insurance-ready annotated PDF report
  • Available as standalone service or enrolled program
  • Rapid response prioritization for enrolled clients

Real Estate

Pre-Listing Property Documentation

When preparing to sell, comprehensive aerial documentation gives prospective buyers an authoritative view of your estate that ground-level photography simply cannot provide. Aerial imagery is among the most impactful marketing assets available to residential sellers.

Our pre-listing documentation package provides a complete set of high-resolution aerial images from multiple altitudes and angles — suitable for use in MLS listings, brokerage materials, and printed marketing collateral.

  • Full exterior and estate aerial photography session
  • Multiple altitude perspectives of all structures
  • Neighborhood context and proximity aerial imagery
  • Pool, outbuildings, and landscape documentation
  • Lot boundary and acreage perspective imagery
  • High-resolution files delivered for print and digital use
  • MLS-ready image package included
  • Optional: 360° aerial tour imagery available on request

Periodic

Seasonal Property Reviews

For homeowners who prefer less frequent monitoring, seasonal reviews provide structured aerial documentation on a quarterly or semi-annual basis. Each review captures how your property transitions across seasons — documenting changes that may indicate developing concerns before they require significant attention.

Seasonal reviews are an excellent entry point for owners who have not previously engaged professional property monitoring. They provide a clear baseline record and a structured schedule of future visits.

  • Two to four scheduled visits per year
  • Full-property aerial documentation each visit
  • Seasonal change and condition comparison imagery
  • Drainage, runoff, and grading observation notes
  • Tree canopy and landscaping change documentation
  • Annotated PDF report with each visit
  • Secure image archive maintained across visits
  • Option to upgrade to monthly program at any time
