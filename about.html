Who We Are

Professional Aerial Oversight for the Properties You Value Most

Woods Aerial Monitoring was founded to serve a specific need: East Texas homeowners who take pride in the upkeep of their properties and who understand that proactive monitoring is simply good stewardship. Whether you own an estate near Lake Hideaway, a primary residence in Tyler, or a second home in the piney woods of Smith County, your property represents a significant investment — one that deserves regular, professional attention.

Our operations are built around a straightforward principle: thorough aerial documentation, delivered consistently, with no disruption to your household. We operate quietly, professionally, and with complete respect for the privacy of your home and your neighbors.

Using commercial-grade drone equipment operated by licensed and insured pilots, we capture high-resolution aerial imagery of your property on your selected schedule. Every visit is organized, documented, and stored — giving you a clear, running record of your property's condition across time.

We are not a technology company or a photography service. We are a property monitoring service that employs aerial technology to do the work more thoroughly and more efficiently than any ground-based inspection could accomplish. Our reports are organized, legible, and delivered in a format that is immediately useful — whether you share them with your insurance carrier, your contractor, or simply keep them on file.

Our Commitments

The Standards We Hold Ourselves To

01

Operational Discretion

We conduct every flight with care for your privacy and the privacy of adjacent properties. Our pilots follow established corridors and maintain professional conduct throughout every visit.

02

Comprehensive Documentation

We do not take shortcuts. Each visit includes systematic coverage of all primary structures, rooflines, drainage areas, and perimeter features according to a documented checklist.

03

Licensed & Insured Operations

All operations are conducted by FAA Part 107 certified pilots carrying full commercial liability insurance. We carry the credentials your property and your neighborhood deserve.

04

Local Knowledge

We know East Texas. We understand the seasonal weather patterns, the types of properties in the communities we serve, and the concerns that matter most to homeowners in this region.

05

Responsive Communication

When something is flagged during a visit, you hear from us promptly. We communicate clearly, without alarm, and always with your best interests as a property owner in mind.
